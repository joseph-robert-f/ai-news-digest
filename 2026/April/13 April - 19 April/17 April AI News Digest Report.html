NVIDIAIsing: first open AI model family for quantum computing

14–16 April 2026 · Primary source: NVIDIA Newsroom

NVIDIA released Ising, an open-weights model family for quantum error correction and calibration, under Apache-style terms via Hugging Face and build.nvidia.com. Ising Calibration is a 35B-parameter vision-language model that compresses multi-day calibration workflows to hours. Ising Decoding variants are 3D CNNs that outperform the open-source pyMatching benchmark by up to 2.5× in speed and 3× in accuracy. Early adopters include Fermilab, Harvard, Lawrence Berkeley National Lab, IQM, and the UK NPL.
