OpenAICodex ships inside the ChatGPT mobile app on iOS and Android — preview, all plans

14 May 2026 · Primary source: OpenAI

OpenAI moved its agentic coding product, Codex, into the ChatGPT mobile app on iOS and Android as a preview, available across all plans including Free and Go. Architecturally interesting: the rollout uses a secure relay layer that keeps trusted machines reachable across devices without exposing them directly to the public internet, with active session state and context synced wherever a user is signed in. From the phone you can start, unblock, review outputs, approve commands, or change models while files, credentials, permissions, and local setup stay on the machine where Codex is operating; screenshots, terminal output, diffs, and test results stream back to the device in real time. OpenAI says 4 M+ weekly active users are already on Codex; Windows host support is “coming soon” (Mac-hosted sessions only at launch).

EuropeCNBC: energy costs threaten to derail Europe’s AI competitiveness with the U.S. and China

18 May 2026 · Primary source: CNBC

A CNBC report published today consolidates what was already a brewing structural concern: European industrial electricity prices last year ran roughly 2× U.S. levels and ~50% higher than China’s and India’s, per IEA data, and CBRE forecasts data-center capacity costs in Europe’s five largest markets (Frankfurt, London, Amsterdam, Paris, Dublin) to rise 12% in 2026.
